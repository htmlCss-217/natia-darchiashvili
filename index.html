    • Here’s Everything You Need to Travel the World


    Figuring out how to travel the world is no easy feat, especially when you’re trying to do it on a budget or planning world travel with a family.There are many factors to keep in mind when evaluating your trip, including what to do before leaving and your budget. To help you get started, we’ve put together a guide on how to travel the world, including everything from how to start looking into your trip to how to save money once you’re abroad.

    If you want to travel the world, there are some planning measures you want to take first to ensure you have durable luggage and the proper documents — passport and possibly visas — and figuring out how much time off you’ll need.

    Working while traveling can be tricky, but there are a few steps you can take that don’t involve quitting your job to travel the world.Start by saving vacation days if you work at a company that pays out for these dates. Those looking to trade in vacation days can also check out companies like PTO Exchange, which lets companies buy back unused vacation days so employees can convert these days into travel, health benefits or charity credits.
